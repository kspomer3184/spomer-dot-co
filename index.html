My name is Kyle Spomer, and I'm a student at Florida Polytechnic University, as well as a Cast Member at Walt Disney World. I've made this website is to give updates about what's going on in my life, as well as to show off some of the projects I've worked on.

I have considered myself a maker for as long as I can remember; whether it was the DIY circuits kit that my grandmother gave me when I was six years old, the love for cooking that I've had since I was 10, the windmill design competition that I won in 8th grade, or the passion for woodworking that I've had for the last 3 years. I live for the design and construction process. It's such a rewarding experience to follow something from just a kernel of an idea, to multiple iterations of building, and in the end being able to have a final product that does something worth doing.

My love for making brought me to that path that I am on today. I am currently a senior in Computer Engineering at Florida Polytechnic University, a brand new state university solely focuses on science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Florida Poly takes a very innovative approach to a STEM education through small class sizes and project based curriculum. My choice to attend Poly has brought about so many opportunities for which I am endlessly grateful. You can read more about my decision and journey here.
