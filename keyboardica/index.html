Keyboardica

HackRiddle 2017

The keyboard whose fame far outweighs its abilities

Keyboardica is probably the least successful hackathon project that I've even been a part of. But somehow, it ended up being a great example about what hackathons are all about.

The pressure seemed pretty high upon returning to Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University for my 3rd time at HackRiddle. Just last year, my team and I gained a repuation as the toaster guys(read more about Toastifai here). Everyone had really high hopes that we would make some awesome hardware hack again. The problem was that we had absolutely no ideas. We spent probably the first 3 or 4 hours "brainstorming". Since absolutly no worthwhile ideas were coming to us, we decided just to have fun with it this time. We had a bunch of hardware and a toy keyboard, and if we could make a toaster smart, then why not a keyboard?
